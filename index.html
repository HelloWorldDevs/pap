Meet Dr. Michael Pap

I have been practicing dentistry for over 12 years and I have been the owner of Westside Family Dental since 2008. I am devoted to helping people of all ages by providing the highest level of quality care in a comfortable, clean and stress-free atmosphere. I graduated with a biology degree from John Carroll University in 1998 where I also participated in the soccer program (four year letter winner and two time all OAC selection) and the basketball program (four year letter winner and advanced to the Elite Eight). I obtained my Doctor of Dental Surgery Degree (DDS) from The Ohio State University in 2002.

I am very pleased to announce that Westside Family Dental was named the "Best of Lakewood" in the Dentistry category by the US Commerce Association and also awarded the "Talk of the Town" award for excellence in customer care presented by the Talk of the Town News.

Today, I live in Rocky River with my wife and 3 children. My wife and I are very happy to live and be part of the Greater Cleveland Community; we are both very actively involved in various local organizations and enjoy spending time with our friends and family. I am proud to be the owner of Westside Family Dental, and cordially invite you and your family to visit my office, meet my welcoming staff and to be a part of our exciting dental practice.
